FAQ


Do you have multiple locations? Can I order from either location?
Hart Davis Hart Wine Co. (“HDH”) sells retail wine in Portland, Oregon, and Chicago, Illinois. You may place orders from either location. All wine offered for sale through https://shop.hdhwine.com/ will be fulfilled through our Portland, OR, warehouse. All wine offered for sale through https://hdhwine.com/retail/ will be fulfilled through our Chicago, IL, warehouse. Please contact sales@hdhwine.com with any questions.

I am a long time HDH buyer. Do I need to create a new account for shop.hdhwine.com?
Yes. Please contact sales@hdhwine.com if you need assistance. You will also need to update your credit card information if you would like to keep one on file.

Does HDH ship wine domestically?
HDH does not ship wine domestically. Wine purchased from our Chicago location must be collected at 1511 W 38th St. by you or an agent designated by you to collect your wine on your behalf. Wine purchased from our Portland, OR, location, will be delivered to/collected by Portland Wine Storage, Inc. (“PWS”), 306 SE Ash Street, Portland, Oregon 97214, as your agent per the terms and conditions of sale related to the purchase of wines through our Oregon location.

Can I combine orders from both HDH locations?
Unfortunately, we are not able to combine orders from different warehouses; you will have to arrange separate pickups for wines at each location.

Can HDH ship internationally?
HDH will be happy to assist international buyers with shipments outside of the United States. Please contact customer service at customerservice@hdhwine.com

Do I owe sales tax for my purchase?
For HDH Chicago wines, HDH is required to collect 10.25% sales tax on all domestic orders. For HDH Portland wines, HDH is not required to collect sales tax on domestic orders. For more tax information, please consult with your accountant or tax attorney as HDH does not provide any tax advice.

Who is PWS?
Portland Wine Storage is an industry leader in wine storage and logistics.

How will I know when my pre-arrival wines are in-stock?
Once your completed pre-arrival or futures order is collected by/delivered to PWS, you will receive an email notification. Once received by PWS, your orders will be available for you or your agent to pick up.

How long will you store my wine for free?
Once your HDH Portland order is transferred to PWS, you will have 30 days of free storage. PWS will collect storage fees for any orders not picked up within that timeframe. At HDH Chicago, you will have 30 days of free storage. HDH will collect storage fees for any orders not picked up within that timeframe.

Who can I contact with further questions?
All shipping and handling questions: Portland Wine Storage - info@portlandwine.com
All wine questions or recommendations: Retail Sales Department - sales@hdhwine.com
Questions regarding payment: Accounting - accounting@hdhwine.com
Everything else: Customer Service - customerservice@hdhwine.com
